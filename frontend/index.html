About Shri Shyam Public School

Building character, nurturing dreams, creating leaders

Our History

Established in 1998, Shri Shyam Public School has been a beacon of educational excellence in Kushalpura for over 25 years. Founded with the vision of providing quality education accessible to all, our school has grown from a modest beginning to become one of the most respected educational institutions in the region.

Our Vision

To create a learning environment that fosters intellectual growth, moral values, and social responsibility, preparing students to become confident, capable, and caring citizens of tomorrow.

Our Mission

  • Provide holistic education that develops mind, body, and spirit
  • Foster critical thinking and creativity
  • Instill moral values and ethical principles
  • Prepare students for higher education and career success
  • Build strong community connections

Principal's Message

"Education is not just about academic excellence; it's about shaping character and building future leaders. At Shri Shyam Public School, we are committed to providing an environment where every child can discover their potential and achieve their dreams. Our dedicated faculty, modern facilities, and proven curriculum ensure that our students are well-prepared for the challenges of tomorrow."
- Mrs. Sunita Sharma, Principal

Document Submission

4

Fee Payment

Pay the admission fee and complete the enrollment process

Eligibility Criteria

Class I

Age: 6 years as on March 31st

Documents: Birth certificate, Aadhar card, photographs

Classes II-V

Requirements: Previous class mark sheet

Test: Age-appropriate assessment

Classes VI-VIII

Requirements: Previous class mark sheet (minimum 60%)

Test: English, Hindi, Mathematics

Classes IX-XI

Requirements: Previous class mark sheet (minimum 70%)

Test: Subject-based entrance test
